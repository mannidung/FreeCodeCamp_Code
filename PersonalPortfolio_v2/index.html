Local Weather App
Have you ever been too lazy to turn your head away from the computer screen and toward the window to look what weather it is outside? Me too! This web application will show you your local weather and is designed to spare you from the tedious task to look for yourself. It also includes some pre-defined cities to demo the transition between different forecasts.

This project is a part of the front end certification at FreeCodeCamp.
